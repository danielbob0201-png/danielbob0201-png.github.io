I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://www.ust.hk/), working with [Professor Junxian He](https://junxianhe.github.io/) in the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkust-nlp.github.io/). I completed my B.Eng. at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Machine Learning**. I am particularly interested in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Developing reasoning capabilities and RL-based improvement methods for large language models.
- **Vision-Language Models (VLM) Hallucination**: Investigating and mitigating hallucination problems in VLMs, particularly in chart understanding tasks.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Exploring the inner mechanisms that drive truthfulness in large language models.
