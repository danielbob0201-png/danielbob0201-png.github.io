I am a first-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://www.ust.hk/), working with [Professor Junxian He](https://junxianhe.github.io/) in the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkust-nlp.github.io/). I completed my B.Eng. at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) in June 2024, where I was awarded the **Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship**.

My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Machine Learning**, with the goal of building more capable, reliable, and interpretable language models.

## Research Interests

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Developing methods to synthesize verifiable reasoning data and improve logical and mathematical reasoning in large language models via reinforcement learning.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)**: Investigating perception bottlenecks and hallucination mechanisms in VLMs, particularly for chart and document understanding.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Exploring the internal representations of LLMs that encode truthfulness, and developing principled methods to improve factual accuracy.
